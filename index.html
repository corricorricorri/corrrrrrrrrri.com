If you're reading this, it means I've given into the pressure of having a presence online. But this time I wanted to keep it simple; no images, no color, simple text. In all honesty, I think I'm a bit tired of the whole "look at my work" thing. I'm really an ideas guy at heart; like Nico said, "I don't do too much talking these days." I'm dying of boredom in corporate America™, so this is my bat signal for the creative directors or studios to find (and hire) me. The term multi-hyphenate gets thrown around a lot these days, but I really do it all.
